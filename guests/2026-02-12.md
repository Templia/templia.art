- **Logo**: mureco.jpg
- **Logo Position**: left

Open daily: 9:00–17:00

- **Logo**: parque-del-jaguar.webp
- **Logo Position**: right

The park connects walking trails, bike paths, miradors, and public beaches, allowing visitors to move through spaces once considered sacred in Maya cosmology — where nature, history, and daily life converge.
