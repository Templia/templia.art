Retreat Overview

  • Name: A Journey Through Time: Inner Anxiety Healing Aligned with the Mayan Calendar

  • Format: Diagnostics of Human Aura Via Bio-Well
  • Shamanic Work

  • Free time
  • Framing: Anxiety reduction, Wellness & self-development retreat, not clinical therapy
  • Target Audience: Women and Men seeking deep transformation and guided reflection
