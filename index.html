Templia Art

Aldea Zama · Tulum, Mexico

Mayan-Inspired Luxury Stay in Tulum

Templia Art is a Mayan-inspired luxury villa in Aldea Zama (Luum Zama), Tulum, Quintana Roo, Mexico. The property features a private pool, jungle garden, Mayan firepit, steam shower, full kitchen, and two bedrooms accommodating up to 4 guests.

Each stay at Templia Art includes a personalized Maya Tzolk'in sacred calendar journey — a day-by-day itinerary built around the ancestral Maya day signs (nawales) that fall across your arrival, full day, and departure. Activities include cenote swims, Tulum ruins at dawn, cacao ceremonies, Temazcal sweat lodges, and fire rituals.

Nearby: Parque del Jaguar (1,000-hectare reserve), Museo Regional de la Costa Oriental (Mureco), Gran Cenote, Cenote Calavera, and the Tulum archaeological zone.

Minimum stay: 3 nights. Check-in after 4:00 PM. Check-out by 11:00 AM. No parties, no pets, no smoking.
