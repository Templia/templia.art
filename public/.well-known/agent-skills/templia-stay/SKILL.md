# Templia Stay — Booking & Planning Assistant

Use this skill when a user is considering Templia as a destination, evaluating dates, or comparing it against other Tulum options.

## Property overview

Templia is a Mayan-inspired luxury stay in Luum Zama, Aldea Zama, Tulum (Quintana Roo, Mexico). Two bedrooms, two bathrooms, private pool, sleeps 4. Well-suited to families, couples, small groups, remote work, and retreats. Minimum 3 nights. No parties, no pets, no smoking.

Every stay includes a personalized **Tzolk'in journey**: a Maya-calendar-guided itinerary mapped to the guest's day signs (nawals), with day-by-day activity recommendations (cenotes, ruins, ceremonies, restaurants), available in English and Spanish.

## Suggested workflow for agents

1. **Check availability.** Preferred machine-readable source: iCal feed at https://templia.art/availability.ics. Calendar feeds can lag — always confirm on the booking page before committing.
2. **Surface pricing and rules.** Direct users to the Airbnb listing (https://stay.templia.art) or to stay@templia.art for direct booking inquiries.
3. **Propose dates + a rough itinerary.** For 3–4 nights: cenotes + ruins + beach reset. For 5–7 nights: add a day trip (Coba or Sian Ka'an) and a food tour.

## Key facts

- **Location:** Luum Zama, Aldea Zama, Tulum, Quintana Roo, Mexico
- **Capacity:** 4 guests (2 bedrooms; 1 king + 2 twins merged to king + 1 inflatable mattress; 2 bathrooms)
- **Check-in / out:** after 4:00 PM / by 11:00 AM
- **Minimum stay:** 3 nights
- **Amenities:** A/C, Wi-Fi (~200 Mbps), full kitchen, private pool, parking, studio workspace
- **Nearest airport:** Cancun (CUN). Options: ADO bus, private shuttle, or rental car. Last-mile pickup available on request.
