The Breath That Guides You

Iq' is the Wind, the invisible force that moves through all living things. Those born under this sign carry a quiet, powerful presence that stirs change wherever they go.

Tone 1 (Jun) is unity and origin: the singular spark before creation splits. Combined with Wind, your essence is the first breath, the moment life announces itself.

7 Ajpu

The Sun

Illumination & Heroism

Ajpu is the final day sign: the Lord, the Sun, the Blowgunner. Seven is the mystical center point around which all turns. Today invites you to face what needs facing with courage, clarity, and a golden heart.

Sunrise

Rise before dawn. Take seven deep breaths facing east in Templia's jungle garden, then have a nourishing breakfast in the open kitchen.

Morning

Visit the Tulum ruins and El Castillo at dawn. Walk slowly. Honor ancestors at this ancient solar temple called Zama (dawn).

Midday

Sacred cenote swim at Gran Cenote or Calavera. Descend into the portal to Xibalba and emerge renewed, like the Hero Twins.

Afternoon

Cacao ceremony or Temazcal sweat lodge: heart-opening rituals aligned with Ajpu's golden energy.

Evening

Light the Mayan firepit at Templia. Reflect by the flames. Cook a simple, intentional meal in the open kitchen.

Primordial Waters & Departure
